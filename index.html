Game Info

This is a turn based game. During the player's turn they may move around the screen within the bounding circle. If they see an enemy, they should try to shoot it by left clicking the mouse on the canvas in the direction of that enemy. The player's turn will end once a bullet is fired and that bullet comes in contact with something or the player presses the "End Turn" button. (only 1 shot per round, make it count!) During the enemies turn the random walkers will move around the screen. If they get close enough to the player they will attack. (make sure you think about your placement) Once the timer at the bottom right hits 0 the players turn will begin again. Once you have less than 10 enemies remaining, during the player's turn you may move freely.

  • Player: Blue square
  • Player Health: Red rectangle
  • Walls: White rectangles
  • Enemies: Green squares
  • Bounding circle: White ring

Controls

  • WASD: moves the plyaer
  • Left Click: shoots in direction of mouse
